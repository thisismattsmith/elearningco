## Rise or Storyline: Making the Right Choice

You've decided to invest in custom eLearning. Now you face a critical decision: which authoring tool should your course be built in?

If you're considering Articulate (the industry standard), you have two main options: **Rise** and **Storyline**. Both are excellent tools, but they serve very different purposes and come with different price points, timelines, and complexity levels.

This guide will help you choose the right tool for your specific project.

## Quick Decision Framework

**Choose Rise if you need:**
- Fast deployment (3 weeks or less)
- Mobile-friendly courses
- Simple, clean design
- Lower budget ($5,500-$7,500)
- Easy future updates
- Template-based efficiency

**Choose Storyline if you need:**
- Complex interactions
- Custom branching scenarios
- Software simulations
- Complete design control
- Game-based learning
- Advanced assessments

Still not sure? Keep reading for the complete comparison.

---

## What is Articulate Rise?

**Rise** is a web-based, rapid authoring tool designed for creating modern, responsive eLearning courses quickly.

**Best For:** Compliance training, onboarding, policy courses, awareness training, and any content that needs to work seamlessly across devices.

**Key Philosophy:** Template-driven efficiency with modern design built-in. Focus on content, not design decisions.

---

## What is Articulate Storyline?

**Storyline** is a powerful desktop application for creating highly customized, interactive eLearning experiences.

**Best For:** Software training, complex simulations, branching scenarios, game-based learning, and situations requiring complete creative control.

**Key Philosophy:** Complete freedom and flexibility. Build anything you can imagine (but it takes longer).

## Side-by-Side Comparison

### Design & Appearance

**Articulate Rise:**
- ✅ Modern, clean aesthetic built-in
- ✅ Fully responsive (automatically adapts to device)
- ✅ Contemporary templates and blocks
- ❌ Limited customization options
- ❌ Template-based look (can feel similar to other Rise courses)
- **Result:** Professional courses with minimal design effort

**Articulate Storyline:**
- ✅ Complete design control
- ✅ Custom layouts and styling
- ✅ Unique, branded experiences
- ❌ Requires design skills or mockups
- ❌ Responsive design requires extra work
- **Result:** Fully customized look, but requires more design time

**Winner:** Rise for speed and mobile, Storyline for customization

---

### Interactions & Interactivity

**Articulate Rise:**
- Built-in interaction blocks:
  - Tabs and accordions
  - Flip cards
  - Labeled graphics
  - Timelines
  - Sorting activities
  - Flashcards
- All interactions are pre-built and template-based
- Quick to implement but limited options
- Can't create custom interactions

**Articulate Storyline:**
- Unlimited custom interactions:
  - Drag-and-drop activities
  - Click-and-reveal with custom triggers
  - Branching scenarios with variables
  - Simulations and software walkthroughs
  - Game mechanics (scoring, levels, badges)
  - Complex decision trees
- Complete control using triggers, variables, and states
- Can build anything, but requires technical knowledge

**Winner:** Rise for standard interactions, Storyline for custom complexity

---

### Development Time

**Articulate Rise:**
- **Simple Course:** 1-2 weeks
- **Standard Course:** 2-3 weeks
- **Complex Course:** 3-4 weeks
- Fast because of templates and limited design decisions
- Less back-and-forth on visual design
- Quick to make updates

**Articulate Storyline:**
- **Simple Course:** 3-4 weeks
- **Standard Course:** 4-6 weeks
- **Complex Course:** 6-12 weeks
- Slower because everything is custom
- More design decisions and revisions
- Visual mockups and approvals needed
- Updates take longer

**Winner:** Rise by a significant margin

## Rise + Storyline: Using Both

Sometimes the best solution is using **both tools** in the same training program:

**Example: Comprehensive Sales Training**

**Rise Modules:**
- Module 1: Company and product overview (Rise)
- Module 2: Sales process overview (Rise)
- Module 3: **Sales conversation simulation** (Storyline)
- Module 4: CRM system training (Rise)
- Module 5: **Objection handling practice** (Storyline)
- Module 6: Resources and references (Rise)

**Result:** Fast, cost-effective development for straightforward content, with custom Storyline modules where high interaction is critical.

**Cost Impact:**
- 4 Rise modules: $7,500 each = $30,000
- 2 Storyline modules: $12,000 each = $24,000
- **Total: $54,000** vs. **all Storyline: $72,000+**

## Common Misconceptions

### "Storyline is always better because it's more powerful"

**Reality:** More power doesn't mean better results. Rise courses often have higher completion rates because they're easier to navigate and work better on mobile. Power is only valuable if you need it.

### "Rise courses all look the same"

**Reality:** While Rise uses templates, courses can look quite different through:
- Brand colors and logos
- Custom images and graphics
- Content structure choices
- Interaction type selections
- Video integration

### "Storyline is too complex for us"

**Reality:** You don't build it yourself - we do. The question isn't "can you use Storyline" but "do you need Storyline's capabilities?"

### "Rise is just for simple courses"

**Reality:** Rise can handle complex content. It's the *interactions* that are simpler, not the content depth. You can teach complex topics in Rise effectively.

## Making Your Decision

Ask yourself these questions:

**1. Mobile Requirements**
- Will learners access on phones/tablets?
- Is mobile the primary device?
- **If yes to either:** Strongly favor Rise

**2. Interaction Complexity**
- Do you need branching scenarios?
- Are custom interactions essential?
- Do you need simulation practice?
- **If yes to multiple:** Favor Storyline

**3. Timeline**
- Do you need launch within 4 weeks?
- Is speed critical?
- **If yes:** Strongly favor Rise

**4. Budget**
- Is budget under $10,000?
- Is cost a primary driver?
- **If yes:** Favor Rise

**5. Future Updates**
- Will content change frequently?
- Do you need easy updating?
- **If yes:** Favor Rise

**6. Design Control**
- Do you have specific brand requirements?
- Need completely custom look?
- **If yes:** Consider Storyline
