commonMistakes:
  - "Information dump instead of welcoming experience"
  - "Too much compliance, not enough culture"
  - "Generic content that doesn't reflect your organisation"
  - "Forgetting to explain the unwritten rules"
  - "Not showing who people are, just what they do"
bestPractices:
  - "Focus on culture and 'how we work' not just 'what we do'"
  - "Include 'meet the team' profiles with personality"
  - "Explain unwritten rules and social norms explicitly"
  - "Structure for just-in-time learning (day 1, week 1, month 1)"
  - "Make it a reference resource, not one-time content"
