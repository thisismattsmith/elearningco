category: "Operational & Technical"

topics:
  - "Product overview and positioning"
  - "Key features and functionality"
  - "Benefits and value proposition"
  - "Use cases and applications"
  - "Target customers and personas"
  - "Competitive positioning and differentiation"
  - "Pricing and packaging models"
  - "Technical specifications explained"
  - "Integration and compatibility"
  - "Implementation and onboarding"
  - "Common customer questions"
  - "Objection handling responses"
  - "Demonstration and proof techniques"
  - "Product roadmap communication"
tier1Example: "We have product specs and positioning. We need training that helps sales and support have confident customer conversations."
tier2Example: "We have product materials but they're feature-focused. We need training on benefits, use cases, and objection handling."
tier3Example: "Our team can't explain why customers should buy our product. We need comprehensive product training from features to value."
commonMistakes:
  - "Listing features without explaining benefits"
  - "Not teaching when the product is right (and when it's not)"
  - "Missing competitive positioning context"
  - "Not preparing for common objections"
  - "Making it too technical or not technical enough"
bestPractices:
  - "Teach benefits and outcomes, not just features"
  - "Include real customer use cases and applications"
  - "Show competitive positioning honestly"
  - "Prepare for actual objections your team hears"
  - "Make it role-specific for different audiences"
