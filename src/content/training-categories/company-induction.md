commonMistakes:
  - "Making it generic instead of site-specific"
  - "Not including actual site photos and layout"
  - "Information overload instead of need-to-know focus"
  - "Not addressing the questions people are too embarrassed to ask"
  - "Forgetting to make it a reference resource"
bestPractices:
  - "Use actual photos and videos of your site"
  - "Create interactive site map with clickable locations"
  - "Focus on critical safety and essential operations first"
  - "Make it a reference people can return to when stuck"
  - "Include the practical stuff: where's the bathroom, where do I park"
