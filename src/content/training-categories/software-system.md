commonMistakes:
  - "Showing every feature instead of focusing on workflows"
  - "Not showing your actual system configuration"
  - "Making it generic instead of role-specific"
  - "Not explaining why the system helps them"
  - "Treating it as one-time event instead of ongoing resource"
bestPractices:
  - "Show your actual configured system, not defaults"
  - "Teach workflows people do daily, not every feature"
  - "Make it role-specific for different users"
  - "Include 'the system nobody actually uses' prevention"
  - "Create ongoing reference, not just launch training"
